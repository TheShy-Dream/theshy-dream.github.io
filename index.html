About me

I am a postgraduate student majoring in Educational Technology at Zhejiang Normal University, class of 2022. My main research areas include intelligent education, affective computing, causal inference, large language models, and diffusion models. So far, I have published 7 papers, hold two national invention patents, and have presided over and participated in five projects.
